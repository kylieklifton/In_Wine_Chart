## Data Source
Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) Wine Statistics
https://www.ttb.gov/regulated-commodities/beverage-alcohol/wine/wine-statistics

## Finding
U.S. wine taxable withdrawals have fallen every year since 2020, dropping 20% from 743 million gallons to 594 million gallons in 2024.
